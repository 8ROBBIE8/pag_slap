Overview

Pull was added on January 1, 2022, and is a slap-costing glove in Slap Battles, costing 250. This glove has a swirly mix of different mesmerizing colors, almost illusive. The handle, like most other gloves, has a void black casing. The texture is called Trippy Texture.

Passive

Pull's passive, "Haul," will fling the slapped user the opposite way. For example, if the user slaps a player pointing to the void, they will be flung into the center as shown below.

GLOVE TIP: The only way you can kill a player with this glove is by standing near the edge and tricking players into going the wrong way.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Can be useful near edges for easily killing players.
  • Can trick other players.
  • Somewhat a counter to Spy.
  • Can diminish the need for flicking.

Cons

  • You have to turn around in order to even hit them into the void.
  • Most players will know what the glove the person is using and its ability.
  • The glove is not very powerful unless you are near the edge of an island.
  • Getting used to how the glove works can be difficult for some players, since the slap direction is inverted.
  • Sometimes you can haul a player in an undesired spot.
  • Practically useless if you unlocked Boomerang, as it has higher power and a combo potential.
  • The glove can easily be countered by most other gloves.

Trivia

  • This, Boomerang, Anchor, and RNG are the only gloves that always send the player in different directions.
  • Even though the glove is called "Pull," Instead of pulling people like the Za Hando glove, it actually just pushes people that it hits behind the user.
